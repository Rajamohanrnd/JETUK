Priest Service Booking

Steps to book your appointment

1. Select the service(s) / pooja(s) and click Next
2. Select the date you want and click Next(If the dates are already booked you will not be allowed to choose the same)
3. Login with your Facebook account or create a new user with us with a valid emailID so that we send the service confirmation(s)
4. Click the confirmation button to show the total cost
5. You need to transfer the amount to the following account with the following reference:
Sort Code: 402 502
Acct No: 6251 6217
Acct Name: Jeeyar Educational Trust UK
Reference: PriestServices.
6. You can as well share your appointment(s) with your family and friends on your social media
7. You can CANCEL the appointment 1 day before the scheduled date with out any charges
8. You can CANCEL your appointment by visiting back to our website and logging in with your user ID and under “My Appointments” you can cancel.

Note: Available only to residents of United Kingdom

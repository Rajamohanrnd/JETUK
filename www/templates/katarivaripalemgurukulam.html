Jeeyar Gurukulam, Katarivaripalem


  • Katarivaripalem is located in Vetapalem Mandalam, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh. This school was established in May 2007 for the benefit of fishermen children. This school was built when tsunami hit the area.
  • It is a non-residential school. Free food, education, study material, uniforms and health care are provided to all the children.
  • 152 students are studying in this gurukulam as of Apr 2017.
